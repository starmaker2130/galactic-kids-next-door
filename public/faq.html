FAQs
What is the ETA estimate at the bottom of the confirmation page?
The Expected Time of Arrival or ETA provides our members with an estimate for when they should expect to receive their completed production(s). Apps that require at least one human review of productions by default take about an hour to process. Depending on the nature of the material being produced, various content reviews must be made, from fully automatic reviews concluded in a matter of seconds to longer human-involved reviews.
Which Apps require Human-Involved reviews?
Certain categories of Apps produce content that can be used in ways that violate our Terms of Use. These must be systematically reviewed by human beings in our Ethics Department. Most Voice Apps and Video Apps on our platform require at least one human review. For a full list of Apps that go through our human-involved review process click here.
What are Voice Apps?
Voice Apps are applications that produce content featuring voices that resemble those of human beings. Current Trending Apps in this category are "AVATAR SAM" and "AI THE LABEL".
What are Video Apps?
Video Apps are applications that produce content featuring film footage produced by a DARIA our A.I. Footage ranges from entirely "generated" imagery to footage filmed by devices remotely embodied and controlled by DARIA. Current Trending Apps in this category are "D. CINEMA" and "HONEYPOT".
